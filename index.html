Myntra Product Intelligence Dashboard (End-to-End Data Analytics Project)

Project Overview: This is a complete end-to-end data analytics case study built on a large Myntra fashion dataset (~526,000 products). The objective was to analyze product performance, pricing strategies, and customer engagement to generate actionable business insights. The project follows a full analytics pipeline including Data Cleaning (Python), Business Analysis (SQL), and Visualization (Power BI).

Business Objective:

  • Understand product and category performance in an online marketplace
  • Analyze pricing and discount strategies
  • Identify factors influencing customer engagement (ratings & reviews)
  • Detect brand dominance and supply concentration

Dataset Information:

  • Source: Kaggle – Myntra Fashion Dataset
  • Size: 526K+ products
  • Major challenge: 300K+ missing values in ratings & reviews

Tools & Technologies: Python (Pandas, NumPy), SQL (MySQL), Power BI, Excel

Phase 1: Data Cleaning & Exploration (Python):

  • Removed irrelevant columns (URLs, descriptions)
  • Handled 300K+ missing values
  • Fixed pricing inconsistencies (original vs discounted price)
  • Created new feature: Discount Percentage
  • Performed EDA (outliers, distribution, correlation)

Phase 2: Data Analysis (SQL):

  • Category-wise product distribution analysis
  • Brand dominance and inventory share
  • Discount strategy comparison
  • Relationship between price, discount, and ratings
  • Used SQL window functions for ranking

Phase 3: Dashboard (Power BI):

  • KPI tracking: Total Products, Brands, Avg Ratings
  • Category distribution and brand analysis
  • Price vs Rating insights
  • Customer engagement tracking

Key Insights:

  • Western Wear & Indian Wear drive highest engagement
  • Few brands dominate marketplace inventory
  • Discounts do not guarantee better ratings
  • Ratings remain stable (~4.1–4.2)
  • Premium segments controlled by fewer strong brands

Business Implications:

  • Opportunity for niche category expansion
  • Focus on brand trust instead of heavy discounts
  • Optimize inventory based on high-performing categories

E-commerce Funnel & Conversion Analysis

Project Overview: This project analyzes user behavior across an e-commerce funnel to identify drop-offs, conversion rates, and revenue leakage using SQL and Power BI.

Problem Statement:

  • Identify major drop-off points in funnel
  • Analyze conversion rates across stages
  • Understand user purchase behavior
  • Improve revenue through optimization

Dataset Overview:

  • ~3 Million user interaction records
  • Event types: Product View, Add to Cart, Purchase

Key Business Questions:

  • Where do users drop off?
  • What are conversion rates?
  • How long does purchase take?
  • Which products have low conversion?

Analysis:

  • Tracked user journey across funnel stages
  • Calculated stage-wise conversion rates
  • Measured time-to-purchase
  • Compared product performance

Key Insights:

  • 97% drop at View → Cart stage
  • Cart → Purchase conversion (~32%) strong
  • Average purchase time ~95 hours
  • High traffic ≠ high conversion

Business Recommendations:

  • Improve product page UX
  • Use retargeting strategies
  • Focus on high-converting products
  • Optimize top-of-funnel engagement
